Our Mission

Our mission at Bakal-AreaNet is to provide a comprehensive platform where individuals can explore and engage with a range of powerful analysis and design tools to enhance their understanding of structural behavior and optimize design solutions.



What We Offer


Axial Tension Analysis and Design:

Dive into our calculators that enable users to assess the behavior of structural elements under tensile forces, optimizing designs for maximum strength and performance.


Axial Compression Analysis and Design:

Explore our tools that aid in evaluating the stability and load-bearing capacity of components under compressive forces, ensuring robust and reliable structural designs.


Bending Analysis and Design of Members:

Uncover the intricacies of beam and column behavior with our analysis tools that covers deflections, stresses, and critical load points for effective design decisions.


Shear Strength Evaluation of Structural Members:

Delve into the world of shear forces in structural elements and optimize designs for increased resistance against lateral forces with our specialized tools.
